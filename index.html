Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add family members to my tree?

To add family members, simply log in to your account and navigate to the "Add Members" section. You can add members directly to your family or categorize them based on their mother and father's side. Fill in the required information, and your family tree will start growing.

What is the benefit of categorizing family members based on their parents' families?

Categorizing family members based on their parents' families helps you create a more comprehensive and organized family tree. It makes it easier to trace your lineage, connect with relatives on both sides, and maintain a clear family history.

Can I invite family members to collaborate on our family tree?

Yes, you can! Family Tree allows you to invite family members to collaborate on your family tree. Simply send them an invitation through the app, and they can join in, contribute, and help expand your shared family history.

Is my family tree information private and secure?

We take your privacy and security seriously. Your family tree information is stored securely and is only accessible to you and the family members you invite. We do not share your data with third parties, and you can control who has access to your family tree.
